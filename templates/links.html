University Information

Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam Technical University (AKTU)

University Ordinances

The university ordinances for the B.Tech program cover all academic rules and regulations, including course structure, examination patterns, and degree requirements. These are updated periodically by the academic council.

  • **Attendance:** A minimum of 75% attendance in lectures, tutorials, and practicals is mandatory to be eligible for end-semester examinations.
  • **Examinations:** The evaluation is based on a mix of internal assessments (sessional exams, quizzes, assignments) and an external end-semester examination.
  • **Promotion:** A student is promoted to the next year based on a minimum SGPA and a set number of earned credits, as defined in the latest ordinance.

AKTU follows a 10-point relative grading system. The letter grade and its corresponding grade point are as follows:

Credit System

The total credit requirement for the award of a B.Tech degree is typically around **160 credits**, distributed across eight semesters.

  • **Theory Courses:** Typically carry 3 or 4 credits.
  • **Lab Courses:** Usually have 1 or 2 credits.
  • **Projects & Internships:** Carry a significant number of credits in the final years.
  • **Non-Credit Courses:** Some courses are mandatory for passing but do not contribute to the SGPA/CGPA calculation.
