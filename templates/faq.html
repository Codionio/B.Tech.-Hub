Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to the most common questions about B.Tech Hub.

1. Is the SGPA Calculator accurate and updated with the latest AKTU rules?

**Yes.** Our calculator is our top priority. We constantly monitor AKTU's official ordinances and grading policies to ensure our tool is 100% accurate. When the university makes a change, we update our system immediately.

2. Is B.Tech Hub free to use?

Our core features, including the SGPA Calculator, Syllabus Viewer, and the Resource Hub, are completely **free** for all students. We plan to introduce a 'Pro' version in the future with advanced features like multi-semester CGPA tracking and performance analytics.

3. How are the study resources (notes, PYQs) collected?

Our resources are a mix of officially available materials and high-quality, curated content from a community of students and educators. We strive to provide the most relevant and helpful materials to aid in your exam preparation.

4. I found an error or have a suggestion. How can I contact you?

We would love to hear from you! Your feedback is crucial for improving the platform. Please visit our **Contact Us** page to send us a message, and our team will get back to you as soon as possible.
