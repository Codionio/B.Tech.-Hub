Product Overview

A quick look at what B.Tech Hub offers for students.

What is B.Tech Hub?

B.Tech Hub is an all-in-one academic companion for engineering students at AKTU. It provides syllabus access, university regulations, SGPA/CGPA calculators, and academic resources — all in a single platform. With a clean interface and easy navigation, you can track your academic progress effortlessly.

Why Choose Us?

  • 📚 Access semester-wise syllabus for all branches.
  • 🧮 Accurate SGPA & CGPA calculators following AKTU norms.
  • 📊 Track academic performance with simple visual tools.
  • 📤 Export reports as PDF/Excel for records.
  • 🔍 Easy-to-use interface with fast navigation.

Our Mission

Our mission is to simplify the academic journey for engineering students by providing a centralized platform that eliminates the hassle of scattered information. We aim to save time, reduce stress, and help students focus on learning.
