Documentation

A complete guide to the features and functionality of the B.Tech Hub platform.

Getting Started

Welcome to B.Tech Hub! This documentation will walk you through the basics of using our platform. Our primary goal is to provide a seamless and intuitive experience. To begin, simply navigate using the main menu. The homepage dashboard provides quick access to the four main pillars of our platform: Syllabus, SGPA Calculator, University Info, and Resources.

Features Overview

Syllabus Hub

Select your engineering branch and academic year to instantly view the official, up-to-date AKTU syllabus in a clean PDF format. No more searching for the correct version.

Advanced SGPA Calculator

Our most popular tool. Select your year, branch, and semester to get a list of subjects. As you enter your marks, the corresponding grade is displayed in real-time. Click "Calculate" to see your SGPA on a professional performance dashboard, complete with charts and a detailed report.

Resource Center

This section provides a structured list of subjects based on your selection. Each subject is linked to a repository of notes, previous year question papers (PYQs), video lectures, and important questions to aid in your studies.

University Info

A centralized hub for all official university information, including the latest ordinances, the official grading system, credit requirements, important university links, and recent news and updates.

Technical Information

The B.Tech Hub platform is built using a modern technology stack to ensure performance, reliability, and a great user experience. The backend is powered by Python with the Flask framework, the frontend is styled with Tailwind CSS for a fully responsive design, and dynamic content is handled by vanilla JavaScript. The live visitor counter is powered by a persistent PostgreSQL database.
