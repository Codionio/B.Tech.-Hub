Our Solution

Addressing the core challenges of student life.

The Problem: Academic Chaos

The life of an engineering student is filled with scattered information:

  • Outdated syllabus PDFs from various sources.
  • Clunky university portals that are hard to navigate.
  • Unreliable SGPA calculators that don't follow official rules.
  • Wasted hours searching for notes and PYQs before exams.
  • Difficulty in tracking academic progress semester over semester.

The B.Tech Hub Solution

We provide a single, unified platform that solves these problems:

  • One Source of Truth: A centralized, official syllabus for every branch and year.
  • Powerful Tools: An advanced, accurate SGPA calculator with a performance dashboard.
  • Organized Resources: A structured library of notes, PYQs, and video lectures.
  • User-First Design: A clean, fast, and mobile-friendly interface.
  • Clarity and Confidence: Empowering students to take control of their academic journey.
